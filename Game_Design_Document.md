# Ocean Reef CO2 - Game Design Document

## 🌊 Core Gameplay Loop
The central mechanic is the **"Predator Boost System"**: killing enemies to gain an upward boost.
You cannot freely swim upward indefinitely. Instead, you:
- Dive / glide downward
- Target enemies (gas phantoms or corrupted fish)
- Strike → absorb energy → launch upward
- Each enemy acts like a temporary platform + jump trigger

Think of it like:
- A mix of platforming + chaining kills mid-air.
- Rhythmic gameplay: attack → boost → aim → attack again.
- Missing targets causes you to fall into dangerous zones (acid, heat currents, abyss predators).

## 🧠 Movement Feel (Crucial)
The “strange platforming” becomes your identity:
- Movement is floaty but intentional.
- No traditional “jump button”.
- Dash = main movement tool.
- Enemies = your “platforms”.
- Momentum matters: The faster you chain kills → the higher you go.
- **Player Fantasy:** “I’m not swimming… I’m hunting my way upward.”

## 🔥 Abilities (Unlocked Over Time)
**Early Game**
- Basic dash attack
- Small boost from enemies

**Mid Game**
- **Thermal Vent Dash:** Dash through vents → massive launch upward
- **Chain Lock:** Slow down time briefly to aim next target

**Late Game (Avatar Form)**
- **Blade fins:** Multi-hit attacks
- **Energy trails:** Damage enemies behind you
- **Temporary free-flight:** Reward after big combos

## 🌍 Level Design
Each area reflects the story:

1. **🪸 Bleached Reef (Tutorial)**
   - Broken coral = static platforms.
   - Few enemies → teaches boost mechanic safely.
2. **🌫️ Acid Currents**
   - Water damages you slowly.
   - You must keep moving via enemy chaining.
3. **🌊 Open Abyss**
   - Almost no terrain.
   - Pure skill-based enemy chaining.
4. **🔥 Thermal Zones**
   - Rising heat currents act like vertical highways.
   - Combine environment + enemies for movement.
5. **🏭 Carbon Citadel (Final Area)**
   - Mechanical + organic hybrid.
   - Enemies become more artificial.
   - Tight, intense sequences.

## 🎭 Narrative Integration
Story is told through the environment and audio, not just cutscenes:
- Background visuals (dead reefs, skeletons, machines).
- Voice echoes from **Hatmehyt** during gameplay.
- **Karu’s** power growing visually as you progress.
- As you get stronger: The ocean becomes more hostile, but you become more unnatural and powerful.

## ⚔️ Boss Concept Example
**“The Boiling Leviathan”**
A massive gas-serpent.
- Dash between its segments.
- Chain attacks to climb its body.
- Reach the head for the final blow.

## 🎨 Visual Identity
- **Color Palette:** Neon blue (life) vs sickly green/yellow (pollution).
- **Enemy Design:** Ghost-like enemies made of smoke and light.
- **Environment:** Background constantly in motion (currents, particles).
